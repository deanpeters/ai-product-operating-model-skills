# Evaluate an AI Initiative

Use this entry path to decide whether a specific AI opportunity should be explored, validated, funded, launched, scaled, constrained, paused, or stopped.

## Entry Modes

- **Guided:** begin with the material decision and consequence.
- **Context dump:** inspect proposals, research, architecture, evaluations, and governance artifacts before asking gaps.
- **Best guess:** label assumptions, reduce authority or exposure, and prioritize the next evidence-producing test.

## Sequence

1. Run [`aipom-opportunity-framing`](../skills/aipom-opportunity-framing/SKILL.md) unless a credible problem frame already exists.
2. Run [`aipom-outcome-value-map`](../skills/aipom-outcome-value-map/SKILL.md) to expose the causal value chain.
3. Use [`aipom-bet-charter`](../skills/aipom-bet-charter/SKILL.md) to establish the owner, constraints, and next test.
4. Add economics, dependencies, data, behavior, evaluation, autonomy, and accountability components proportionate to the decision.
5. Run [`aipom-initiative-readiness-review`](../skills/aipom-initiative-readiness-review/SKILL.md) before a material launch, scale, sensitive-data, or autonomy decision.

## Context Handoff

Preserve the opportunity, alternatives, evidence, causal assumptions, economics, dependencies, behavior, context, data, evaluation, authority, controls, incidents, adoption, owners, disagreements, and unresolved questions.

## Stop and Escalation Rules

- Do not use a demo as value, feasibility, or readiness evidence.
- Do not let expected value compensate for a critical control gap.
- Do not infer specialist approval from workshop participation.
- Constrain scope or pause when recovery, accountability, or evidence is insufficient.

## Completion

Finish with a proceed, proceed-with-constraints, remediate-and-re-review, pause, or stop recommendation; the accountable decision; evidence conditions; owners; expiry; and the next review trigger.

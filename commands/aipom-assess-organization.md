# Assess an Organization

Use this entry path to move from an organizational maturity question to an owned operating-model roadmap.

## Entry Modes

- **Guided:** begin with scope and ask one consequential question at a time.
- **Context dump:** inspect supplied documents and pre-populate the evidence ledger before asking gaps.
- **Best guess:** label assumptions, use low confidence, and cap unsupported maturity at `2 — Emerging`.

## Sequence

1. Run [`aipom-operating-model-assessment`](../skills/aipom-operating-model-assessment/SKILL.md).
2. Route the weakest consequential conditions to the relevant category advisors; do not run all seven automatically.
3. Use focused component skills only where they can change the diagnosed condition.
4. Run [`aipom-operating-model-roadmap`](../skills/aipom-operating-model-roadmap/SKILL.md) to sequence containment, learning, establishment, and scaling.

## Context Handoff

Preserve scope, operating levels, evidence, confidence, disagreements, critical gaps, assumptions, decisions, owners, outputs, unresolved questions, and next actions. Do not make users repeat evidence between skills.

## Stop and Escalation Rules

- Separate organization maturity from initiative readiness.
- Do not average away legal, privacy, security, safety, accountability, or rollback gaps.
- Do not claim a complete assessment until all intended canonical questions are covered.
- Route specialist decisions to the accountable specialists.

## Completion

Finish with a seven-category profile for a full assessment, priority conditions, constrained decisions, named interventions, accountable owners, evidence milestones, and a dated roadmap review.

# AIPOM v0.5 — Synthetic Public Preview

This is the first public preview of AI Product Operating Model Skills: a platform-neutral library of 41 skills for assessing, designing, and improving how organizations choose, build, evaluate, govern, and learn from AI-powered products and AI-assisted product work.

## Evidence Label

`v0.5` is structurally validated and synthetically forward-tested. It has not yet been validated through representative organizational field sessions, and demonstrated adoption or impact has not been established. All worked examples are synthetic. The library does not replace accountable specialist decisions or establish compliance.

## Included

- 41 canonical skills across seven operating-model categories and four operating levels
- 35-question organizational assessment, evidence rubric, and maturity scoring model
- Three guided entry paths: organization assessment, initiative evaluation, and product-team workflow redesign
- Templates plus synthetic worked and weak examples for every skill
- Platform-neutral canonical archive and Codex-compatible archive
- Versioned packages with SHA-256 checksums
- Public feedback, safety-reporting, and field-validation infrastructure

## Validation Performed

- 41 skills and 35 assessment IDs validated
- Repository-relative links checked
- Source presence and traceability checked
- Six representative skills synthetically forward-tested and hardened
- Clean-checkout validation and packaging verified
- Archive contents and SHA-256 checksums verified

# AIPOM v0.6 — Five Persona Starter Packs

AIPOM v0.6 turns the complete 41-skill AI Product Operating Model library into five approachable, job-specific starting experiences. Heads of Product, CTOs, Product Operations practitioners, Product Managers, and Team Leads can now begin with a recognizable decision and a bounded working project instead of navigating the entire catalog.

## Evidence Label

`v0.6` is structurally validated and synthetically forward-tested. All five Starter Packs also pass automated clean-room cold-start checks. Representative human field validation and demonstrated organizational adoption or impact have not yet been established. All worked examples are synthetic. The library does not replace accountable specialist decisions or establish compliance.

## What Is New

- Five plain-English persona guides with distinct responsibilities, authority boundaries, and routes through the library
- Five end-to-end journeys for product direction, technical readiness, organization assessment, initiative evaluation, and workflow redesign
- Five distinct Starter Packs:
  - Head of Product — set AI product direction
  - CTO — establish the AI technical readiness bar
  - Product Operations — assess the organization
  - Product Manager — evaluate an AI initiative
  - Team Lead — redesign a product-team workflow
- A guided and command-line Starter Pack generator with Bash and PowerShell wrappers
- Native Codex `$aipom-start` and Claude Code `/aipom-start` entry points generated from one maintained workflow
- Dependency-aware manifests, lockfiles, safe regeneration, deterministic archives, and checksums
- Six executive adoption packages and a clearer role-, problem-, and decision-led public front door

## Included Downloads

- Canonical platform-neutral AIPOM library
- Codex-compatible skill archive
- Five self-contained Starter Pack archives, each supporting Codex and Claude Code
- SHA-256 checksums for all seven archives

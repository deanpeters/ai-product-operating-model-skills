# Choose Your AIPOM Starting Path

You do not need to read or run all 41 skills. Start with the decision you need to make, then use the practitioner guide to prepare the people, evidence, and authority the work requires.

## Compare the Paths

| Path | Choose it when | Typical shape | Primary result |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Assess an Organization](journeys/ASSESS-AN-ORGANIZATION.md) | The issue spans several teams, initiatives, or operating-model conditions | 4–7 facilitated hours across 2–4 sessions, plus preparation | Evidence-based profile and owned improvement roadmap |
| [Evaluate an AI Initiative](journeys/EVALUATE-AN-AI-INITIATIVE.md) | A material decision is pending for one AI opportunity or initiative | 4–8+ facilitated hours across 2–4 sessions, proportionate to consequence | Proceed, constrain, remediate, pause, or stop decision |
| [Redesign a Product-Team Workflow](journeys/REDESIGN-A-PRODUCT-TEAM-WORKFLOW.md) | The unit of change is one recurring decision or productive motion | 4–7+ facilitated hours plus a real-use pilot | Redesigned decision cycle and evidence-based adoption decision |

The time ranges are planning guides, not guarantees. Each path also includes a 60–90 minute smallest useful version for teams that need to scope the decision or produce the next evidence before undertaking the full journey.

## 1. Assess an Organization

Choose this path when leaders need an evidence-based view of how the organization currently chooses, builds, evaluates, governs, and learns from AI product work.

Read the [practitioner journey guide](journeys/ASSESS-AN-ORGANIZATION.md), then use the [facilitation and agent command](../commands/aipom-assess-organization.md).

Expected result: a seven-category maturity profile, critical gaps, prioritized interventions, owners, and an operating-model roadmap.

## 2. Evaluate an AI Initiative

Choose this path for a specific AI idea, pilot, launch, scale, sensitive-data, autonomy, or funding decision.

Read the [practitioner journey guide](journeys/EVALUATE-AN-AI-INITIATIVE.md), then use the [facilitation and agent command](../commands/aipom-evaluate-initiative.md).

Expected result: a framed opportunity, owned bet, evidence and readiness profile, critical constraints, and a proceed, constrain, remediate, pause, or stop decision.

## 3. Redesign a Product-Team Workflow

Choose this path when a recurring product-team decision or productive motion is slow, inconsistent, context-poor, or being changed through AI assistance.

Read the [practitioner journey guide](journeys/REDESIGN-A-PRODUCT-TEAM-WORKFLOW.md), then use the [facilitation and agent command](../commands/aipom-redesign-workflow.md).

Expected result: an observed current-state motion, explicit human-AI responsibilities, a tested redesign, measures, and a reusable playbook.

## If You Are Unsure

- Choose organization assessment when the problem spans several teams or categories.
- Choose initiative evaluation when a specific investment or launch decision is pending.
- Choose workflow redesign when the unit of change is recurring work performed by a product team.

Start smaller when evidence, ownership, or capacity is weak. A focused path that changes one decision is more useful than running the entire library as a checklist.

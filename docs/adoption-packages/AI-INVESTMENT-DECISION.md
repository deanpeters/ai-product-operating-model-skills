# Executive Adoption Package: AI Investment Decision

Use this package to decide whether an AI opportunity deserves discovery, validation, continued funding, pivot, scale preparation, deferral, or rejection.

## Sponsor Decision

> What level of capital and capacity is justified by the problem evidence, causal value, economics, responsibility, feasibility, reversibility, and current uncertainty?

The package separates learning capital from scaling capital. It should prevent a prototype, demo, executive preference, or vendor promise from becoming investment evidence by default.

## Best Fit

- Comparing an intake of AI ideas
- Funding discovery or validation
- Challenging a solution-first or vendor-first proposal
- Revising investment after new evidence
- Creating stage-specific continuation, pivot, pause, and stop discipline

For a launch, scale, sensitive-data, or autonomy decision, use [Initiative Readiness](INITIATIVE-READINESS.md) after the investment case is framed.

## Sponsor and Participants

The accountable sponsor is the investment or portfolio decision owner. Include the Product Manager, customer or domain representation, design, engineering, data, finance, operations, and governance specialists proportionate to consequence.

## Evidence to Prepare

- Customer or workflow problem evidence and affected perspectives
- Current alternatives and non-AI options
- Outcome baselines, frequency, consequence, and current cost
- Existing prototype, experiment, evaluation, or adoption evidence
- Full lifecycle cost and economic assumptions
- Platform, vendor, data, talent, integration, and switching dependencies
- Responsibility, reversibility, control, and recovery constraints

## Indicative Engagement

**Planning range:** 4–7 facilitated hours across 2–4 sessions, plus preparation.

1. **Frame the opportunity:** use [AI Opportunity Framing](../../skills/aipom-opportunity-framing/SKILL.md) to establish the condition, affected actors, alternatives, consequence, and next uncertainty.
2. **Map causal value:** use the [AI Outcome and Value Map](../../skills/aipom-outcome-value-map/SKILL.md) to connect proposed behavior to customer, product, economic, and risk outcomes.
3. **Choose the investment posture:** use [AI Use-Case Triage](../../skills/aipom-use-case-triage/SKILL.md) when comparing opportunities or selecting explore, validate, defer, or reject.
4. **Charter the bet:** use the [AI Bet Charter](../../skills/aipom-bet-charter/SKILL.md) to name ownership, scope, constraints, evidence, next test, and stop conditions.
5. **Deepen proportionately:** add the [AI Economic Case Builder](../../skills/aipom-economic-case-builder/SKILL.md) and [AI Platform Dependency Audit](../../skills/aipom-platform-dependency-audit/SKILL.md) for material funding or vendor exposure.
6. **Set the gate:** use [AI Investment Stage Gates](../../skills/aipom-investment-stage-gates/SKILL.md) to define entry, continuation, pivot, scale, pause, stop, and exception decisions.

Do not require a full economic case for a small reversible learning test. Do not use a lightweight bet charter to justify irreversible scale.

## Package Outputs

- Opportunity frame and alternatives
- Outcome and causal-value map
- Evidence, assumption, and uncertainty ledger
- Explore, validate, defer, reject, fund, pivot, pause, or stop recommendation
- Owned bet with scope, constraints, next test, and stop conditions
- Economic and dependency analysis proportionate to commitment
- Stage-specific evidence requirements and next review

## Exit Conditions

The package is complete when the decision owner has selected an investment posture, bounded the permitted spend or motion, assigned the next evidence-producing action, and set a decision trigger. “Promising” is not an investment decision.

## Authority and Caveats

The investment owner decides capital and capacity. Product, technical, finance, legal, privacy, security, safety, risk, and governance authorities retain decisions within their remit. Expected value cannot offset a critical control gap, and the package does not certify readiness or compliance.

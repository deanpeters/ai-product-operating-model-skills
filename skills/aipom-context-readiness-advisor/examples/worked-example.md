# Worked Example

**Synthetic example.** A support assistant gives conflicting refund guidance. Inspection finds three policy sources, no named owner, different effective dates, and access rules that exclude contractors.

The diagnosis is not “insufficient prompt context”; it is conflicting authority and access. The advisor recommends an authoritative source map before building a bounded package. Operations owns policy precedence; legal reviews contract implications; tests cover current, expired, and contractor-access cases.

# Synthetic Worked Example: Evaluation Practice Adoption

The scorecard tracks Product Managers exposed to the learning path, scorecards used in real gates, calibrated evaluation plans, launch decisions changed by evidence, evaluation-related rework, specialist review burden, and reuse of governed cases. Results are segmented by role and workflow. Participation is high, but only teams with manager reinforcement change gate decisions. The organization scales manager routines, revises the self-serve course, and stops reporting prompt volume as success.

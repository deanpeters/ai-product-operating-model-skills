# Synthetic Worked Example: Escalation Preparation

Three observed cases show that specialists spend 8–22 minutes locating entitlement, severity, and prior-action facts across four sources. The official workflow omits a recurring clarification handoff and the exception path for conflicting severity data. Waiting, not reading, causes most delay; missing entitlement facts cause most rework. The redesign target is a cited brief plus explicit conflict flag, not full escalation automation. Baselines include preparation time, clarification rate, missing-critical-fact rate, and reopen rate.

# Worked Example: Discovery Interview Synthesis

**Synthetic example.** AI may transcribe approved recordings, extract candidate observations with timestamps, and cluster similar statements. A researcher checks source fidelity and exceptions. AI may not infer demographic attributes, discard contradictory evidence, or decide the opportunity priority.

The Product Manager recommends priorities; the research lead approves the evidence synthesis and remains accountable for research quality. Missing consent or source links stops processing. Review burden, missed contradictions, synthesis time, and decisions changed are measured during a three-study pilot.

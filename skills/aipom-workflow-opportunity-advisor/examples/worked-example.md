# Worked Example

**Synthetic example.** A product group compares meeting summaries, interview synthesis, evidence review, and roadmap formatting. Evidence review is weekly, delays funding decisions, and repeatedly loses source provenance. It has bounded inputs and a human decision forum.

The advisor recommends redesigning evidence review first, not roadmap formatting. Baselines are decision cycle time, reopened decisions, missing citations, and review burden. Product Operations owns a current-state motion map before any automation.

# Synthetic Worked Example: Escalation Routing

The current cycle loses time gathering facts and resolving severity conflicts. The redesign uses AI only to create a cited brief and flag conflicts. Specialists verify critical facts and decide routing; incident commanders resolve severity disputes. A matched historical comparison measures preparation time, clarification, omission, reopen rate, and review burden. The pilot shows faster preparation but no improvement in routing until the conflict rule changes, so the team revises the decision cycle before scaling the AI step.

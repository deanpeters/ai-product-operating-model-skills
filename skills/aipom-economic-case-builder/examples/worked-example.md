# Synthetic Worked Example: Escalation Briefs

The case models reduced specialist preparation time only when that capacity improves resolution throughput. It includes model use, integration, evaluation, review, monitoring, support, and source-governance costs. Downside assumes low adoption and higher review burden; upside assumes stable omission rates and capacity conversion. Sensitivity shows review time—not model price—drives break-even. Scale requires sustained preparation savings, no increase in reopen rate, and total review burden below 40% of time saved.

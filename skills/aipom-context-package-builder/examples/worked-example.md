# Worked Example: Pricing Decision Context

**Synthetic example.** A product team packages the current pricing policy, approved segment definitions, margin guardrails, research summaries, and prior pricing decisions for an AI-assisted pricing review.

The finance policy owns economic constraints; the product strategy memo owns segment priorities. A stale sales deck is included only as historical context and cannot override either. Customer contracts, personal data, and unapproved forecasts are excluded. The package expires after the quarterly pricing review or any finance-policy change and is tested against one normal proposal, one low-margin exception, and one conflicting-source case.

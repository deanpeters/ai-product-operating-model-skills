# Worked Example: Regional Home-Services Platform

**Synthetic example.** The prior assessment found scattered pilots, missing behavior and autonomy boundaries, conflicting knowledge sources, weak portfolio gates, and training without applied-practice evidence.

| Horizon | Motion | Evidence milestone | Owner/decision |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30 days | Contain autonomous scheduling changes; charter two scale candidates; map source authority | Unsafe action disabled, two comparable charters, policy owner named | Operations director and portfolio leader |
| 90 days | Establish behavior contracts, autonomy boundaries, and one evidence-based portfolio review | Tested stop flow, representative cases, explicit continue/pause/stop decisions | Product and governance owners |
| 180 days | Standardize the proven support workflow and context package across two teams | Repeated use, lower rework, no critical control failures | Product Operations |
| 365 days | Scale only practices with measured decision or customer impact; retire weak pilots | Outcome and reuse evidence plus retirement decisions | CPO |

The roadmap defers enterprise training expansion until role competencies and applied practice are defined. It does not schedule all 41 skills.

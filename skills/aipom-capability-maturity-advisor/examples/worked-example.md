# Worked Example

**Synthetic example.** Ninety percent of Product Managers completed AI training, but portfolio proposals still lack evidence, evaluation, and stop criteria. Two teams use strong bet charters with coaching.

The advisor rates organization-wide capability as emerging, recommends a role-specific investment-decision competency plus coached application to the next three proposals, and converts the proven review motion into a reusable skill. Evidence is proposal quality and changed funding decisions—not attendance.

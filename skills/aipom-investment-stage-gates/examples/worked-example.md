# Synthetic Worked Example: Case-Brief Investment

The exploration gate requires an evidenced workflow problem, named owner, non-AI comparison, and bounded test. Validation requires representative omission tests, measured preparation time, reviewer burden, and privacy approval. Limited operation requires monitored fallback and incident ownership. Scale additionally requires stable workflow outcomes, operating cost, adoption evidence, and portability review. A failed critical-fact threshold blocks progression even when time savings are strong. Exceptions expire after one review cycle.

# Worked Example: Weekly Evidence Review

**Synthetic example.** Three product teams repeatedly use a 30-minute review that distinguishes evidence, assumptions, decisions, and next tests. Over eight weeks it reduces reopened decisions and duplicate research.

The converter preserves the four-part evidence ledger, numbered decision options, an escalation when evidence conflicts, and a stop rule when no accountable decision owner attends. It creates a facilitator-readable `SKILL.md`, report template, worked example, weak “status meeting” example, and three evaluation scenarios. Product Operations owns quarterly review and retirement if the motion no longer changes decisions.
